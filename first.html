Recipe #1: Making a Pizza

Time: 15 mins

Serves: 1

Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Base 1
Sause 1 bowl
Cheese 2 cubes

Step 1: Take the Pizza Base and apply sauce all over one side .

Step 2: Spread mashed cheese all over that side and distribute evenly.

Step 3: Put pizza in the oven for 7 mins and then enjoy it hot

Recipe #2: Making a Sandwich

Time: 10 mins

Serves: 1

Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 pieces
Sause 1 bowl
Cheese 1 cube
Vegies(Cut in small) 1 small bowl

Step 1: Take the bread and apply sauce all over one side of each of the two breads.

Step 2: Spread mashed cheese all over the side of sause on one bread and spread it evenly.

Step 3: Put mashed vegies on top of that cheese and put the other bread on top of it. Enjoy!
